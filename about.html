About

I am a French girl living in Montreal (Quebec) since 2018. I worked for 15 years in sales and project management, in the furniture and design industry. As I needed new challenges in my professional life, I recently decided to change my career path. Coding came to my mind and I decided to just jump in and give it a go !

What excites me most about working in web development is being able to create things that have purpose and to directly visualize the result of my work. I am detail-oriented and I value collaboration with both customers and team members. I strongly believe this is the key to a reliable working process and a successful project delivery.

Outside work, I dance and teach Boogie Woogie. Whether by myself, with a dance partner or with a dance crew, I participate in shows and engage in competition. Check out these videos if you feel curious !
